# Meal plan

> A week of dinners that respects your real constraints, not a generic list.

## Goal (the what)

- **Objective:** A 7 day dinner plan.
- **Success criteria:** No main ingredient repeats across the week, and every meal cooks in under 30 minutes.
- **Constraints:** Avoid anything I said I dislike. Keep it to ingredients I can buy locally.
- **Context:** My dietary preferences and anything already in the fridge.

## Loop (the how)

- **Think:** Pick a dinner that fits the rules and uses an unused main.
- **Act:** Add it to the plan with its main ingredient and cook time.
- **Observe:** Check the day against both rules (no repeat main, under 30 minutes).
- **Reflect / Plan:** Swap any day that breaks a rule, fill the next open day.
- **Stop when:** All 7 days pass both rules.

## Starter prompt

```
Plan 7 dinners. Rules: no main ingredient repeats across the week, and every meal
cooks in under 30 minutes. Avoid [dislikes]. Draft the week, then check each day
against both rules and fix any that break them. Show it as a table with meal, main
ingredient, and cook time. Stop only when all 7 days pass both rules.
```
