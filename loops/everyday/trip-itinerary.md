# Trip itinerary

> A day-by-day plan where the logistics actually work, not a wish list of places.

## Goal (the what)

- **Objective:** A 3 day itinerary for a city.
- **Success criteria:** Every stop is within a 15 minute walk of the next, and nothing is closed on the day you visit.
- **Constraints:** No more than 4 stops a day. Leave time for meals.
- **Context:** The city, travel dates, and the kinds of places you want to see.

## Loop (the how)

- **Think:** Choose the next stop that fits the day and is open that day.
- **Act:** Place it, then look up its hours and the walk to the next stop.
- **Observe:** Check hours against the visit day and the distance against the 15 minute rule.
- **Reflect / Plan:** Reorder or swap stops that break a rule, fill remaining gaps.
- **Stop when:** Every entry has confirmed hours and a walk under 15 minutes to the next.

## Starter prompt

```
Plan a 3 day itinerary for [city] on [dates]. Rules: each stop within a 15 minute
walk of the next, and nothing closed on the day I visit it. Max 4 stops a day. Draft
the days, then verify each stop's hours for that day and the walking distance to the
next. Fix anything that breaks a rule. Show hours and walking distance for every
entry. Stop only when all three days pass both rules.
```
