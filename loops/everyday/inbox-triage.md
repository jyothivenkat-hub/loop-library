# Inbox triage

> Get to a clean inbox with a decision logged for every message.

## Goal (the what)

- **Objective:** Clear the last 24 hours of email.
- **Success criteria:** Every email is archived, replied to, or flagged with a reason. Inbox zero for the window.
- **Constraints:** Do not send replies without showing me the drafts first.
- **Context:** My inbox, and who my important senders are.

## Loop (the how)

- **Think:** Read an email, decide its category (archive, reply, flag).
- **Act:** Archive it, draft a reply, or flag it with a one-line reason.
- **Observe:** Confirm the email now has a logged decision.
- **Reflect / Plan:** Move to the next undecided email.
- **Stop when:** No email from the last 24 hours is left undecided.

## Starter prompt

```
Go through my unread emails from the last 24 hours. For each, decide: archive, reply,
or flag. Draft replies for anything that needs one (do not send yet). Flag anything
needing my decision with a one-line reason. Keep going until every email has a
decision. Then show a summary: count archived, the drafts you wrote, and the flagged
list with reasons. Stop when nothing from the last 24 hours is left undecided.
```
