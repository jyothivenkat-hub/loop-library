# Competitive analysis

> Turn a look at competitors into a prioritized, actionable improvement list.

## Goal (the what)

- **Objective:** Analyze competitors and turn it into action.
- **Success criteria:** Competitive analysis of 5 similar tools, 3 key UX gaps in our app identified, and a prioritized list of 8 improvement recommendations with mockup concepts.
- **Constraints:** Every gap and recommendation tied to evidence from the analysis. Prioritize by impact and effort.
- **Context:** Our current app and the 5 competitors to study.

## Loop (the how)

- **Think:** Pick the next competitor or feature area to examine.
- **Act:** Document how they handle it, compare against our app.
- **Observe:** Note where we fall short, gathering evidence for gaps.
- **Reflect / Plan:** Cluster findings into gaps, turn gaps into recommendations, rank them.
- **Stop when:** 5 tools analyzed, 3 gaps evidenced, and 8 prioritized recommendations with mockup concepts are written.

## Starter prompt

```
Conduct a competitive analysis of these 5 productivity tools: [list]. For each,
document how they handle key flows and compare against our app. Identify the top 3 UX
gaps in our app with evidence. Then produce a prioritized list of 8 improvement
recommendations (ranked by impact and effort) with a mockup concept for each. Stop
only when all 5 tools are analyzed, 3 gaps are evidenced, and 8 prioritized
recommendations with mockup concepts are delivered.
```
