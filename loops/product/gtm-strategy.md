# Go-to-market strategy

> A launch plan with personas, pricing, positioning, and a dated 90-day plan.

## Goal (the what)

- **Objective:** Create a go-to-market strategy for a new product.
- **Success criteria:** Target personas, pricing tiers, positioning, and a 90-day launch plan.
- **Constraints:** Ground personas and pricing in real comparable products, not guesses. Keep the 90-day plan to dated milestones.
- **Context:** The product, the market, and any existing customer or pricing data.

## Loop (the how)

- **Think:** Decide the next piece to develop (personas, pricing, positioning, timeline).
- **Act:** Research comparables and draft that piece.
- **Observe:** Check it for evidence and internal consistency with the other pieces.
- **Reflect / Plan:** Reconcile conflicts (e.g. pricing vs persona), fill the next piece.
- **Stop when:** All four pieces exist, are consistent, and the 90-day plan has dated milestones.

## Starter prompt

```
Create a go-to-market strategy for [product]. Deliver: target personas, pricing tiers,
positioning, and a 90-day launch plan with dated milestones. Ground personas and
pricing in real comparable products and cite them. Develop each piece, then check
them for consistency with each other. Stop only when all four pieces exist, are
consistent, and the 90-day plan has concrete dated milestones.
```
